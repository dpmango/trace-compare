  • Senior 3D artist
  • 3D artist

    • In St. Petersburg office (Russia) we are looking for 3D Artist who will be responsible for controlling and leading the process of assets creation.

    Responsibilities:
    • Sculpt, model, and texture props, weapons and characters;
    • Create both high resolution models and final game-ready assets;
    • Work with the Lead Artist and Managers to balance technical quality and art performance;
    • Create great looking art in a variety of styles, both from concept-art and from scratch;
    • Maintain excellent file structure and organization;
    • Work with task tracking tool.
    Requirements:
    • At least 2 years of 3D modeling for gamedev industry;
    • Spicy portfolio;
    • Specialization in props modeling using 3ds Max/Maya, Zbrush;
    • Experience with Photoshop, Substance Painter, Quixel Suite;
    • Expertise in creating Hi-poly models, Low-poly models, UV, baking, and texturing;
    • Understanding of photogrammetry;
    • Ability to work with technical documentation (keen eye for details, adherence to specifications);
    • Understanding of next-generation asset creation pipelines and tools;
    • An exceptional understanding of form, shape, structure, and silhouette in regard to modeling.
    We offer:
    • Loads of great tasks;
    • Assets that make strong impact on the atmosphere of the games we are working on;
    • Easy-going management with desire to invest in promising people;
    • Reliable colleagues;
    • The chance to change the future of the company.
    And even more:
    • Formal employment in accordance with labour code of the Russian Federation;
    • Flexible working schedule;
    • A convenient office close to the metro station;
    • A chance to participate in interesting projects;
    • Opportunities for professional and personal growth;
    • Respectable salary based on job interview results.
    Please send your portfolio to
    job@trace-studio.com
